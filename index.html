About Me:

My Story -

I was born March 20, 1992 in Redmond, Washington, where I lived until just a few months ago. Growing up, I would watch my dad and my older brother build their own computers, and it sparked in me a love and fascination for technology that I still carry with me today. I'm constantly looking for ways to improve myself both as a developer and an individual, and I can't wait to continue my career as an aspiring programmer. Thanks for stopping by.

In my spare time, I enjoy -

  • Hiking - Getting to hike the beautiful trails of Washington state is something I try to never take for granted.
  • Gaming - Whether it's playing a game with a strong story, or competing with friends online, gaming has always been one of my biggest passions.
  • Basketball - After I started playing pickup games with friends and coworkers, basketball became just about my favorite way to get excercise.

And of course..

  • Studying - I understand that i'm still at the beginning of my journey as a dev, and I know the importance of having a Growth Mindset. The tech industry advances at such a rapid pace, I believe it's crucial to have a desire to improve yourself, little by little, each and every day.
